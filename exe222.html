Join Our Academic Team

Victoria University is inviting applications for Lecturers and Senior Lecturers in the Faculty of Science and Technology and the Faculty of Health Science. Be part of a dynamic and forward-thinking institution committed to research, innovation, and academic excellence.

Faculty of Science and Technology

Lecturer - Computer Science

Location: Main Campus

Employment Type: Full-time

Requirements: Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science, prior teaching and research experience.

Responsibilities: Deliver undergraduate courses, supervise projects, engage in research.

Senior Lecturer - Information Technology

Location: Innovation Campus

Requirements: PhD in IT or related field, strong publication record, leadership experience.

Responsibilities: Teach, mentor staff, lead research initiatives.

Faculty of Health Science

Lecturer - Public Health

Location: City Campus

Employment Type: Full-time

Requirements: Master's or PhD in Public Health, relevant experience in teaching and community health research.

Senior Lecturer - Nursing

Location: Health Sciences Campus

Requirements: Registered Nurse, PhD in Nursing or related field, experience in curriculum development.

How to Apply

Submit the following documents via email to hr@vu.ac.ug:

  • Cover Letter
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Academic Transcripts and Certificates
  • Two Reference Letters

Deadline: July 31, 2025

Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews.
